PPAP? No, it's PappaRoti!

If you’re ever craving for dessert or a quick snack for breakfast, PappaRoti is the best choice. Located on Robson Street, it is one of a kind coffee shop in Vancouver. It started as a popular café franchise in Malaysia and has over 400 cafes worldwide including London, Singapore, Dubai and Australia. Today, Vancouver can experience the ‘godfather of all’ buns.

Strawberry with Whipped Cream Bun

The bun is a simple pastry: light and airy on the inside with a crispy buttery flavor outer dome that cracks a little when you break it apart. Each bun’s top layer is piped with coffee caramel before it enters the oven. I have to say, this is by far the best bun I’ve eaten. The most satisfying part is when you order the bun; you can enjoy a side dish of maple syrup and ice cream, strawberries and whipped cream or banana and nutella. Did I also mention they serve JJ bean coffee and custom-blended, loose-leaf Teaja teas? I know!

The space also has a modern industrial interior design. Huge mirrors, wooden tables and mismatched chairs give a warm and vibrant atmosphere. This is the best place to work on your projects, read a novel or grab a seat near the window to watch people cross by Robson Street! Overall, this is a thumb up restaurant from us!
