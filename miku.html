Live in Vancouver? You got to try Miku!

If you enjoy being in a lively, exciting and high quality sushi restaurant, Miku is your answer. The service and the quality of the food on the menu are exceptional. As you enter the restaurant, you will be surrounded by traditional Japanese paintings, an open kitchen, cozy furniture and friendly servers.

Miku's open space design at Waterfront.

When it comes to sushi, Miku is the king of Aburi Oshi Sushi, pressed, sauced and blowtorched sushi and sashimi. Though Miku is not the first to introduce such technique, it is definitely one of the well-known restaurants in town. And Miku does this elaborately. The signature plate consists of 10 pieces of ocean-wise salmon, tuna, shrimp and a selection of chef’s feature of the day. The fish and rice combination is perfect and melts directly in your mouth. Can I get a bottle of Sake on the side, please?

As for dessert, you won’t be disappointed. The famous Opera Cake is a classic. It is a combination of green tea and French Mocha layers, accompanied by hazelnut wafer, azuki bean cream and matcha ice cream. What else can you ask for?

For more information to fill in your belly, please visit Miku restaurant.
