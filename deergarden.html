It’s Sunday at 3pm and you’re craving some comfort food. Has anyone told you about Deer Garden? And no, they don’t serve deer meat. This is a restaurant that often has long line-ups during lunch and dinner.

When you first enter the restaurant, you may be overwhelmed with the menu and the ingredients. But if you’re looking for a local Chinese Cart Noodle experience, then you’ve come to the right place. Deer Garden has a wide selection of Hong Kong Café Style items such as rice bowls, sandwiches, mini hot pots, snacks and set meals. The most popular item is the noodle soup bowls that you write the order yourself and hand the sheet off to the server.

You can select your choice of noodles, home made soup broth and 2 toppings with a choice of beverage (coffee or tea) for only less than $10.

Don’t expect Michelin style noodle. But it’s really a bang for your buck and the portion is huge. It also comes with mushrooms, Chinese Napa cabbage, bean curd skins, green onions and cilantro as toppings.

If you’re ready to hit the comfort food tonight, go make a trip to Deer Garden!
